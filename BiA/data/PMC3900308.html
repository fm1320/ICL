Copyright and License information Disclaimer

This corrects the article "Qualitative Profiling and Quantification of Neonicotinoid Metabolites in Human Urine by Liquid Chromatography Coupled with Mass Spectrometry" in volume 8, e80332.

The chemical structure of clothianidin and N-desmethyl-clothianidin (CM-1) in Figure 1 is incorrect. The correct version of Figure 1 can be viewed here: http://plosone.org/corrections/pone.0080332.g001.cn.tif
